How I work
L1 · Sprint
Discover
I start where the problem lives — not in Figma. Stakeholder interviews, shadowing real workflows, mapping how work actually moves through a system. This sprint surfaces what the brief never says.
Stakeholder interviews Flow mapping Gap analysis
L2 · Sprint
Define & Design
Research becomes structure. I define the system architecture first — hierarchy, states, edge cases — then move into high-fidelity UI. Everything is built to hold under real conditions, not just happy paths.
Wireframes Prototypes Design system
L3 · Sprint
Ship & Validate
Designs don't end at handoff. I walk stakeholders through every interaction, absorb feedback, and iterate until the solution is shippable — not just presentable. Then I track whether it held in production.
Dev handoff Walkthroughs Iteration
